# Prompt Engineering – AI SEO Blog & Content Cluster Generator

Business Type: Coaching Institute  
Location: Bangalore  
Target Audience: Students preparing for competitive exams, academics, and skill-based courses  
Business Goal: Increase local search traffic, build trust, and generate enquiries  

---

## Prompt 1: SEO Pillar Blog (Main Article)

You are an SEO content strategist and professional blog writer.

Create a long-form, SEO-optimized pillar blog for a coaching institute website.

Primary Keyword:
Best Coaching Institute in Bangalore

Blog Requirements:
- Use proper SEO structure with H1, H2, and H3 headings
- Write clear, helpful, and human-readable content
- Include the location (Bangalore) naturally throughout the blog
- Explain services, benefits, teaching approach, and what students should expect
- Add trust-building elements (experience, faculty quality, student support)
- End with a business-focused conclusion encouraging enquiries

Also include:
- Suggested internal links to related supporting blogs
- Clear section flow suitable for a real coaching institute website

Tone:
Professional, informative, and student-friendly.
Avoid keyword stuffing or promotional exaggeration.
